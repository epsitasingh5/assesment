Microsoft Office,or simply office,is a family of client
software, server software,and services developed by
microsoft.It was first announced by Bill Gates on
August 1,1988, at COMDEX in Las Vegas.
Initially marketing term for an office suite,
the first version of office contained Microsoft word,Microsoft excel,
and Microsoft Powerpoint.Over the Years,office applications
have grown substantially closer with
shared features such ass a common spell checker.
Object linking and embedding data integration and
Visual Basic for applications scripting language.
Microsoft also positions office as a development
platform for line of bussiness software under
office Business Applications brand.
